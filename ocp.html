Soil testing and analysis for 480 farmers in the Sene West District by OCP Africa

OCP Africa is one of the leading producer of fertilizer for supply throughtout the African continent and beyound. OCP Africa has what we call farmer school Lab. Over the years this Farmer school Lab have been taking farmers soil sample, analyze the soil sample and give the results to farmers for free as well as taking farmers throuhg Good Agricultural Practices (GAPs). Incollaboration with the Ministry of Food and Agriculture, the OCP farmer School Lab was in the Sene District from 28th of August, 2021 to 3rd of September, 2021. Agric Extension Agents (AEAs) from the Deparpment of Agriculture, Sene West District were engaged in the collection of soil sample from thirty(30) farmers each from sisteen(16) communities. These communities namely: Kwame Danso, Bantama, Wiase, Mframa, Chense Battor, Akenten, Nkwankwadua, Lemu, Drobe, Kofi Djan, Kyeamekrom, Tato Battor, Dwankrom, Menkor, Lassi and Akyeremade were selected at random to as representative for the whole Sene West District. Soil sample taken from the farmers field were then analyzed in three (3) Van with soil texting kits that were brought to the District by the OCP Africa.There was grand durbar in each of the sixteen (16) communities a day after the soil test analysis for a particular community were ready. During the durbar, the AEA in the operational are took the farmers through good Agricultural Practice in maize production after which the test results were given out to the farmers. In one of the durbars at Menkor, the District Director of Agriculture, Mr Matthew Opoku made the farmers aware of the importance of farmers getting their soils tested. he added that it is an opportunity that was brought to their door-step since it cost a lot of money for an individual to test their soils with other institutions. The results contained the following parameter: soil PH, Available P, Exchangeable K, Calcium, Magnesium, Iron, Mangansese, Boron, Copper, Zinc, C.E.C, Total Nitrogen and organic matter. The hardcopy of the test results also contained on the second page recommendations for each of the farmer who had their soils testedaccordion-accordion
